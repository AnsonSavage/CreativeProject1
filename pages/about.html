Who is Anson Savage?


Photo of Anson Savage turning to the dark side and trying Maya. Don't worry, he quickly returned to Blender.

  I am! I'm a Freshman (Junior?) studying Computer Science (pre-animation) at BYU! I enjoy making beautiful art with Blender! Blender is just about the coolest open source project ever created, by the way.

  I also enjoy running, playing the piano, and programming of course! I have an awesome family that does awesome stuff sometimes.

  I strongly dislike beaucracy.

  When I was in 7th grade, I was struck with an overwhelming desire to create lightsaber special effects. And so, that's what I did. I opened up Blender, tried really hard my whole Spring Break to make it work, to no avail. So, I closed Blender and didn't touch it for a whole year...

  The end.

  Except, then I picked it up again a year later to try to make a 3D logo, because that's what 8th graders need to do sometimes. And then I couldn't put Blender down, because it's glorious. But I got kind of compulsive and only did homework, so I really only worked on Blender when school wasn't happening. Which was a serious sin. You should never let homework get in the way of your dreams.
